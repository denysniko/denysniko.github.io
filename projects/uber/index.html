About us

Uber

is an American global publicly company based in San Francisco that created a mobile application of the same name to search, call and pay for cabs or private drivers and food delivery. With the help of the Uber app, a customer reserves a car with a driver and tracks its movement to a specified point. In most cases, drivers use their own cars and those of taxi fleets or partners. In most countries, 67% of the payment goes to the driver and 33% is transferred to Uber.
